About Us

We are the E-Ville Empire based in Essexville, Michigan. Our team has been around for five years and counting. Over those years, we have placed highly in most of our competitions, making it to worlds twice. The students on the team all attend Garber High School and work countless hours to improve and perfect the robot with close to no help from the mentors. The program is non-profit and is made entirely to help give students an idea of what there is to offer in the STEM field. We look forward to many more years of competition to kick some nuts and bolts!

What is FIRST Robotics competition?

FIRST Robotics competition is were high school students and mentors have 6 weeks to build a robot to complete taskes, The game changes every year when the sessen start. While playing the game, the teams have opportunities to earn awards.
